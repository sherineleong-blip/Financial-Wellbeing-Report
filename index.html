In summary

What's working — and where to focus next

Where you stand out

  • RM290,000 combined annual income, including RM18,000/year net rental from the investment property
  • Positive household net worth of RM1,317,000, anchored by two properties worth RM1.5m combined
  • RM460,000 in EPF already built between Sam and Sarah
  • A regular investment habit — RM1,200/month into unit trusts, up +13.0% on capital invested
  • Solid medical & life insurance foundation already in place for the whole family, including the children
  • Clear, specific goals already defined — education and retirement targets are written down, not vague

Where to focus next

  • Build cash reserves from RM20,000 toward a RM60,000 three-month buffer — RM40,000 to go
  • Lift the unallocated savings rate from RM1,537/month (6.4%) toward RM3,500/month
  • Close the combined RM1,620,000 life-insurance gap with a proper needs analysis for Sam and Sarah
  • Separate education investing from retirement investing — right now RM1,200/month is doing both jobs
  • Review unit trust fees, fund overlap and allocation — 3–4% returns may not be enough for either goal
  • Review whether both vehicle loans need to continue, and the investment property's net rental yield
